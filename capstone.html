Industrial ROV System
MARINOVA

Exclusively underwater-focused branch of HYDRONE — a low-cost, tethered ROV built for underwater inspection. Originally developed for UVDC India 2024 as PROJECT AQUA (2nd place globally), then physically realized as a capstone project at a total build cost of 160 USD.

Surface-Relay CommsFloating buoy with VTX & FS-i6 receiver → 3m Cat6 Ethernet tether. Lollipop antenna for smooth FPV video.

Full 6-DoFDJI 920KV BLDC on MG996R servo mounts pivot 0°→90°, achieving complete 6 degrees of freedom thrust vectoring.

WaterproofingM-Seal epoxy + cable glands for wire penetrations; TPU balloons for servos & torch housings.

Passive Safety1 kg cast-iron counterweight → stable low-positive buoyancy. Passively surfaces on power loss.

Environmental SensingpH, salinity, turbidity, dissolved oxygen sensors for underwater environmental monitoring.

Ultra Low CostFull industrial-grade ROV built for just $160 USD.
