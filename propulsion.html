MICKEY
Variable Pitch Dual-Medium Propulsion

HYDRONE's biggest propulsion bottleneck solved: a fixed pitch propeller for air performs poorly underwater at the same RPM, and vice versa. MICKEY allows blade pitch to change dynamically as the vehicle crosses the air-water interface — maintaining thrust and efficiency in both media with the same motor and RPM.

How It Works

  • Air pitch (53° servo rotation): Flatter-optimized for aerial thrust. Servo-actuated control rod escalates inner hub relative to fixed outer hub.
  • Water pitch (68° servo rotation): Wider-optimized for underwater propulsion using the same mechanism.
  • Moisture sensor detects medium change and signals Arduino, which commands the servo.
  • Validated at 50% throttle — structural integrity and sufficient propulsion in both media confirmed.
